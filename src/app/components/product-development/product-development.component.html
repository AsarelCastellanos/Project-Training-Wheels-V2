What Should You Expect?

During the product development phase this summer you are expected to stick with your team, make sure they are all making progress and help them push through all of thier hurdles.

Agile Development

In this course learn how to help your team develop there projects in an effective and effiecent way. Eliminate waste through such practices as selecting only the truly valuable features for a system. It emphasizes the speed and efficiency of development workflow.

Problem Statements

In this course learn what a problem statement is, how to help your team break down there problem in order to find the root of the problem. Learn how to ask your team the right questions in order to make sure they have a solid problem statement.

Design Thinking

Design Thinking is an iterative process in which we seek to understand the user, challenge assumptions, and redefine problems in an attempt to identify alternative strategies and solutions that might not be instantly apparent with our initial level of understanding.
