TXT Creed

I shall lead by example.

I will bring honor to the brotherhood.

I will think outside the box.

I believe in helping my neighbor and community.

I believe leadership is action not position.

I will be a role model to other young males in my community.

I believe nothing is hard just a lot of work.

I will strive to know myself and seek self improvement.

I believe in the habit of doing more than asked for.

I will strive to strengthen my mind body and soul.
