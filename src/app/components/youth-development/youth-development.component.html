What Should You Expect?

This summer you are expected to be able to teach the students what it means to be a part of TXT. You are expected to enforce certain habits and dissuade them from thier old negative habits. Along with a multitude of other things.

What Is The TXT Culture?

TXT culture can be summed up to two things. The Creed and The Pillars. These two encompass what TXT is about and what is taught to everyone who becomes a part of TXT.

The Creed

  • I shall lead by example.
  • I will bring honor to the brotherhood.
  • I will think outside the box.
  • I believe in helping my neighbor and community.
  • I believe leadership is action not position.
  • I will be a role model to other young males in my community
  • I believe nothing is hard just a lot of work.
  • I will strive to know myself better and seek self-imporvement.
  • I believe in the habit of doing more than asked for.
  • I will strive to strengthen my mind, body, and soul.
